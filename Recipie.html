The Best Ever Fudgy Brownies!

The Brownies are the most delicious recipies and I'm sure that this is going to turn up as your favourite!

okayy! Lets move on to the Recipie.

Ingredients:

  • Dark chocolate
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Vanilla Essence
  • Chocolate chunks

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven at 180 degree celcius.
  2. Melt the dark chocolate and add butter to it.
  3. Transfer them into a bowl and mix well.
  4. Add 1 cup sugar and 1 cup flour.
  5. Then add 2 eggs and blend well. you can use a blender too to make it fluffy.
  6. add two table sppon vanilla essence.
  7. Finally add some chocolate chunks, mix well and yeah your batter is ready!
  8. Bake a 180 degree celcius or 350F for 20-25 minutes.

Your yummy Fudge Brownies are ready...!!!
